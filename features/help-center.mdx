The Help Center is a knowledge base where COMPANY_ADMINs publish articles to help users resolve common issues without opening a ticket. Articles are organized into four global categories and tracked by view count.

All authenticated users can read `PUBLISHED` articles. Only COMPANY_ADMINs can create, edit, publish, or delete articles.

## Categories

Articles are organized into four fixed, platform-wide categories:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Account & Profile" icon="user">
    Account setup, profile management, and preferences.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Security & Privacy" icon="shield">
    Passwords, two-factor authentication, and data privacy.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Billing & Payments" icon="credit-card">
    Invoices, payment methods, and subscription management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Technical Support" icon="wrench">
    Troubleshooting guides and technical how-tos.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Retrieve the category list and their UUIDs at any time:

```bash
GET /api/help-center/categories
```

## Article lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create (DRAFT)">
    Submit `POST /api/help-center/articles` with the required fields. Articles always start in `DRAFT` status and are not visible to regular users until published.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit">
    Update draft content at any time using `PUT /api/help-center/articles/{id}`. Updates also work on already-published articles without changing their status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    When the article is ready, call `POST /api/help-center/articles/{id}/publish`. The article becomes visible to all authenticated users and its `published_at` timestamp is recorded.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Unpublish (if needed)">
    To pull an article from public view, call `POST /api/help-center/articles/{id}/unpublish`. The article returns to `DRAFT` status. View counts are preserved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating an article

`POST /api/help-center/articles` — COMPANY_ADMIN only.

<ParamField body="category_id" type="string" required>
  UUID of one of the four global categories. Retrieve IDs from `GET /api/help-center/categories`.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="title" type="string" required>
  Article title. Must be unique within your company.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="content" type="string" required>
  Full article body. Supports Markdown.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="excerpt" type="string">
  Optional short summary shown in article listings.
</ParamField>

```bash
curl --request POST \
  --url https://your-domain.com/api/help-center/articles \
  --header "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_JWT_TOKEN" \
  --header "Content-Type: application/json" \
  --data '{
    "category_id": "a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7890-abcd-ef1234567890",
    "title": "How to reset your password",
    "content": "## Steps to reset your password\n\n1. Go to the login page.\n2. Click **Forgot password**.\n3. Enter your email address and click **Send reset link**.\n4. Check your inbox and follow the link within 1 hour.",
    "excerpt": "A step-by-step guide to resetting your account password."
  }'
```

## Publishing an article

`POST /api/help-center/articles/{id}/publish` — only works on articles in `DRAFT` status.

```bash
curl --request POST \
  --url https://your-domain.com/api/help-center/articles/ARTICLE_UUID/publish \
  --header "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_JWT_TOKEN"
```

## Unpublishing an article

`POST /api/help-center/articles/{id}/unpublish` — returns the article to `DRAFT`. Only works on `PUBLISHED` articles.

```bash
curl --request POST \
  --url https://your-domain.com/api/help-center/articles/ARTICLE_UUID/unpublish \
  --header "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_JWT_TOKEN"
```

---

## Updating an article

`PUT /api/help-center/articles/{id}` — all fields are optional. Works on both `DRAFT` and `PUBLISHED` articles.

<Info>
  Updating a published article does not change its status or `published_at` timestamp.
</Info>

```bash
curl --request PUT \
  --url https://your-domain.com/api/help-center/articles/ARTICLE_UUID \
  --header "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_JWT_TOKEN" \
  --header "Content-Type: application/json" \
  --data '{
    "title": "How to reset your password (updated)",
    "excerpt": "Updated guide for resetting your account password."
  }'
```

---

## Deleting an article

`DELETE /api/help-center/articles/{id}` — soft delete. Only `DRAFT` articles can be deleted.

<Warning>
  Published articles cannot be deleted. Unpublish the article first, then delete it.
</Warning>

---

## Reading articles

`GET /api/help-center/articles` — returns `PUBLISHED` articles to all authenticated users. COMPANY_ADMINs also see `DRAFT` articles from their company.

<Info>
  View counts are automatically incremented each time a `PUBLISHED` article is read via `GET /api/help-center/articles/{id}`.
</Info>

| Parameter | Type | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `search` | string | Search in title and content |
| `category` | string | Filter by category slug: `ACCOUNT_PROFILE`, `SECURITY_PRIVACY`, `BILLING_PAYMENTS`, `TECHNICAL_SUPPORT` |
| `status` | string | `PUBLISHED` or `DRAFT` (COMPANY_ADMIN only) |
| `sort` | string | Sort field and direction, e.g. `-views` for most viewed first |
| `page` | number | Page number (default: 1) |
| `per_page` | number | Items per page (max: 100) |
